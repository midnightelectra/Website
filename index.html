UPCOMING EVENTS

SILENT DISCO

Station 1640, April 21th, 2023 at 9pm

1640 N. Cahuenga Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90028

Step into the world of sonic magic with Midnight Electra and DJ 4MULA’s back-to-back set at Station 1640’s Silent Disco. Known for their curated blends of future house and techno, Electra and 4MULA’s diverse discography comes from a love for high energy crowds and deep connections to their audiences. Whether you’re a die-hard fan of electronic dance music or just looking for a night of entertainment, their debut performance is not to be missed.
